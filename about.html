How is AI related to Human Augmentation?

Human Augmentation and AI are the words of same origin, Human Augmentation means the replication of human behaviour, and AI is the intelligence which the machine is infilled in it to act like a human.

How are they different then?

AI focuses on the goal of using it to design automated systems that replace human cognitive function. ... Intelligence augmentation uses machine learning technologies that are similar to AI, but instead of replacing humans, it aims to assist them.
